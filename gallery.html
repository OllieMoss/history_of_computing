    The Difference Engine was designed by Charles Babbage in the 1820's and completely built by the London science museum in 1991.   • Algorithm for computing Bernoulli Numbers

    Ada met Charles Babbage in 1833 and from then on they became friends, during that time Ada translated a paper Luigi Menabrea an Italian mathematician wrote about the Analytical Engine. Ada added thousands of notes and realised the analytical engine could compute extensive sequences of mathematical operations. She went on to write a sequence that could calculate Bernoulli numbers which is regared by many to be the first computer program.
